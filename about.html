Cao, Long, or Hoàng Long?

This question has been asked a million times. I want to clarify once again: Cao is my family name, and Hoáàng Long is given name which literally means The Golden Dragon. According to Vietnamese custom, I prefer to be called Long in all circumstances even though my name is displayed as Hoang-Long Cao in all international publications.

My name is written as 高黃龍 in traditional characters.

Sports, musical instruments, and sketching

Everyone knows I am physically (and mentally) weak. But it doesn't mean I don't play sports. I play table tennis and badminton quite often. I also practise a Vietnamese martial art called Thuy Phap which immitates the flow of water.




I spent a couple of years learning keyboard. However, I'm currently sticked with pianica (piano + harmonica). Unlike me, my brother spends a lot of time for his music passion. Check out his Metal Rain rock band.




I love sketching. With a pencil and a piece of paper, you can sketch your moods. I'm pretty sure I got this love from my dad who was a civil engineer. I love the way he designs buildings, bridges, and tunnels. To me, he's an architect.
