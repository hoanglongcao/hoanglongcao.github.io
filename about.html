Cao, Long, or Hoàng Long?

This question has been asked a million times. I want to clarify once again: Cao is my family name, and Hoàng Long is given name which literally means The Golden Dragon. According to Vietnamese custom, I prefer to be called Long in all circumstances even though my name is displayed as Hoang-Long Cao in all international publications.

My name is written as 高黃龍 in traditional characters.
