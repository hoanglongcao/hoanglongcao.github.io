I was educated at Can Tho University, Kemi-Tornio AMK (Lapin AMK since 2011), Universite Libre de Bruxelles and Vrije Universiteit Brussel in Mechatronics Engineering. I began my career as a lecturer at Can Tho University. Since 2010, I have spent most of my time abroad for my master and PhD degrees.

I put a lot of effort on popularizing knowledge about robot to society. You may have seen several of my tutorials about how to program microcontrollers and design simple mobile robot platform posted and reposted on many websites. I organised some robot activities for high school students. You may have met me at robot competitions: Robocon Vietnam, Micom Car Rally Vietnam, Robocup Junior Belgium,...
