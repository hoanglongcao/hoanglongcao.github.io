Using ChatGPT to program the social robot Pepper

April 04, 2023

The Pepper robot is designed to interact with humans in a social environment. However, programming the robot to perform various tasks such as recognizing emotions, following commands, and reacting appropriately can be a complex and time-consuming process. The emergence of ChatGPT suggests a new method for programming robots like Pepper to become easier. In this post, I attempt to use ChatGPT in programming the Pepper robot.
